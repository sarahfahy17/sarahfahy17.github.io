Delete the Asterix

Project 3 of 6

This project is a series of posters, Instagram posts and stickers designed for a social justice campaign advocating for more easily accessible mental health care that is free with no exceptions. The asterix next to the word “free” signifies the terms and conditions that currently make mental health care difficult to access.

Supervised by

Adam Cruickshank.

Regent Theatre Publication

Project 4 of 6

This is a pamphlet about the refurbishment of the Regent Theatre in Melbourne, which was nominated in the Victorian Architecture Awards. It highlights the key features that were salvaged and replaced in the building, and the graphics used are decor from the interior of the building.

Supervised by Owen Cramp.

Digital Archive

Project 5 of 6

This project is an archive of artefacts from the Viking age using both found imagery and my own photography. The navigation graphic is inspired by an image on a tapestry that is in the collection. The website is mobile adaptive and uses HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
